Lesson 1 Finding fossil man

Posted on 2018-08-18 | In New Concept English 4

Words

Words Explanation
recount tell someone about something; give an account of an event or experience
saga a long history of heroic achievement, especially a medieval prose narrative in Old Norse or Old Icelandic
flint a hard gray rock consisting of nearly pure chert, occurring chiefly as nodules in chalk

Phrases

Phrase Explanation
read of same meaning as read about
the first people primitive people

Comprehension

  • How can anthropologists learn about the history of ancient peoples who have not left written records?
    • Anthropologists can use sagas that left by storytellers to learn about the history of ancient people.
  • Why did ancient men prefer to use flint for making tools?
    • Because flint is easier to shape than other kinds.

Vocabulary

  • preserve
    • The only way that they can preserve their history is to recount it as sagas–legands handed down from one generation of storytellers to another.
    • Only when the rare china is broken have we realized the emergency to preserve such vulable cultural relics.
  • recount
    • He recounted the concept of entropy as something that is the measure of uncertainty.
  • migrations
    • These legends are useful because they can tell us something about migrations of people who lived long ago, but none could write down what they did.
    • Repid migrations to these ancient continent lead to the irreversible climat change and environmental damage.
  • anthropologists
    • Anthropologists wondered where the remote ancestors of the Polynesian peoples now living in the Pacific Islands came from.
    • Some anthropologists firmly hold the belief that sagas can perfectly explain the history of the Polynesian peoples.
  • remote
    • Knowing that Qichao Liang is one of his remote ancestor who still have great influence on today’s world, he uplifted his spirit and carried on writing his first monography.
  • decay
    • Stone does not decay, and so the tools of long ago have remained when even the bones of the men who made them have disappeared without trace.
    • The role of the extended family has been decaying for some time.
    • Pollution has decayed the surface of the stonework on the front of the cathedral.
    • the smell of decaying meat.
  • without trace
    • My notebook is haunted, every time I put it in my desk, the next morning it will disappear without trace.
